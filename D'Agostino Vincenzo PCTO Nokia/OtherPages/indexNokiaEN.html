Who is Nokia?

Nokia is a Finnish multinational company, and was a global leader in mobile phones for many years. Beyond its historical role in mobile devices, Nokia is now a key player in:
  • Mobile and fixed networks (4G, 5G)
  • Smart cities and industrial automation
  • Partnerships with schools and universities (like my PCTO project)

Its role in this project

Nokia partnered with our school for the ECHO project, offering technical support and guidance to build a robot with AI, voice control, and sensors. This opportunity allowed us students to gain hands-on experience in engineering and innovation.

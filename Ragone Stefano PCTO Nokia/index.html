Nokia: My Experience

What is Nokia?

Nokia Corporation is a company specialized in Telecommunications, once focused on the field of Mobile Telephony.

History

1865

Nokia was founded in Finland by engineer Knut Fredrik Idestam, near Tampere, for pulp production.

1981

Launch of the Nokia Cityman 900, one of the first mobile phones.

1992

Presentation of the Nokia 1011, the first fully digital GSM phone.

2000

Launch of the legendary Nokia 3310, famous for its durability and battery life.

2013

Nokia sold its mobile division to Microsoft, marking the end of an era.

2023

Nokia renewed its logo and focused on telecommunications infrastructure.
